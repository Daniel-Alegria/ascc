American Society of Concrete Contractors

Safety Message

A core value, ASCC is uncompromising in its commitment to health and safety for its membership. As we live out our mission -- Enhancing the Capabilities of Those Who Build With Concrete-- we strive to continually improve safety processes and promote an unwavering commitment to safety. This effort has led us to partner with ClickSafety to provide safety training solutions for our members. We believe this partnership will help our members provide a safe work environment for their employees, a goal of ASCC since its founding.
