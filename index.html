Osha 10 Hour Construction

OSHA-Authorized online course featuring the steps for completing OSHA Outreach Training, a proven way to receive a valid DOL/OSHA 10-Hour Card. This course teaches recognition, avoidance, abatement and prevention of safety and health hazards in workplaces. There is also information regarding workers’ rights, employer responsibilities and how to file a complaint.

Osha 30 Hour Construction

This course is an OSHA-Authorized online course featuring the required steps for completing OSHA Outreach Training and is a proven way to receive a valid OSHA 30-Hour Card. This course covers everything from Electrical Hazard Safety to Fall Protection.

OSHA 30 Hour Voice Authentication (Note this is for NY)

REQUIRED FOR NEW YORK CITY - VA courses were developed in conjunction with NYCDOB to address LL196 online course proctoring requirements. The voice authenticated OSHA courses represent a best practice to ensure the identity of the student throughout the course.

Cal OSHA 10 Hour Construction

This course is designed to help keep California workers safe and is a proven way to receive a valid OSHA 10 Card. This online training course teaches recognition, avoidance, abatement and prevention of safety and health hazards in workplaces. This program also provides information regarding workers’ rights, employer responsibilities and how to file a complaint.

OSHA 10 Hour - Español

Este curso es parte de un programa en línea OSHA alcance que resulte en una válida DOL / OSHA 10-Hour Card. Este curso de formación en línea enseña a reconocer, evitar, la reducción y prevención de los riesgos para la seguridad y salud en los lugares de trabajo. Este programa también proporciona información sobre los derechos de los trabajadores, las responsabilidades del empleador y cómo presentar una queja.
